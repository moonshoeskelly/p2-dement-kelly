Volunteer Your Time

Working Together to Help Build Lives that Work

Volunteers are asked to commit at least 4 hours per shift and are required to wear appropriate dress code which consists of pants, collar-type shirt, and closed-toe shoes. All volunteers must complete an online application.

Goodwill offers many ways to volunteer. See which area suits you best!
